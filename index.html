Nanotechnologies to mitigate global warming

How global warming is related to nanotechnology. 4th may 2010

Well, mitigate means to make something less severe, serious, or painful. It's the article about how nanotechnology can help mitigate the global warming. Nanotechnology has a significant potential to solve or mitigate the problem of global warming. However, one has to approach this issue carefully. Nanotechnology invariably involves use of nanomaterials (nanocatalysts, nanomembranes, nanoparticles, aerogel etc.)
